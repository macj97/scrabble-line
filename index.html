Scrabble

  • Drag a tile onto a space on the board.
  • Tile will not move once it is placed on the board.
  • Tiles can only be placed from left to right, no spaces.
  • Click "Submit Word" when you have made a word.
  • Click "Restart Game" if you want to restart the game.
  • "Score" displays your total score.
  • "Current Word Score" displays your potential score if you play what is currently on the board.
  • If you get a "blank" tile:
    • Before you hit "Submit Word," make you type the letter you want the blank tile to be in the text box below.
    • Text box will only appear when you get a blank tile.
